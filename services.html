The Collection Process

Operational Flow with Green City Disposal & Recycle SDN BHD:

Step 1: Segregation
Separate organic waste into dedicated green bins. No plastics or metals allowed.

Step 2: Specialized Pickup
Sealed bio-waste trucks collect the organic matter to prevent odors in Johor Bahru.

Step 3: Bio-Conversion
Waste is processed into bio-fertilizer or biogas at our Tebrau facility.

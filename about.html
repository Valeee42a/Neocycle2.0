About NeoCycle

NeoCycle is a specialized digital platform dedicated to the management and recovery of Food Waste within the Johor Bahru region.

Food waste accounts for a significant portion of urban landfill volume. Our project, conceptualized at NMUC, provides a high-tech interface to help residents and businesses divert organic waste away from landfills and toward sustainable processing centers.

By digitizing the reporting and collection process, we aim to make Johor Bahru a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable city for the future.

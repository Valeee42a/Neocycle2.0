Our Mission

NeoCycle is a digital waste management initiative conceptualized at Netherlands Maritime University College (NMUC). We focus specifically on the most critical waste stream in Malaysia: Food Waste.

In Malaysia, food waste makes up nearly 45% of municipal solid waste. When left unmanaged, it leads to landfill overflow and environmental pollution. NeoCycle solves this by providing a "Digital Bridge" between the public and professional collection services.

Why Digital Integration?

Inspired by highly efficient systems like Japan's, NeoCycle utilizes QR Code technology to make reporting easy. By scanning a code, users in Johor Bahru can instantly access daily schedules and locate our organic recovery hubs at the Ibrahim International Business District.

Our goal is to transform "waste" into "resources" through smart, localized technology.

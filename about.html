About NeoCycle

NeoCycle is a digital waste management initiative designed for the Johor Bahru community. Our mission is to integrate smart technology with traditional recycling methods to reduce landfill waste.

Developed at NMUC, this platform bridges the gap between residents and local collection centers for Plastic, Metal, Glass, Food, and E-Waste.
